Interesting Questions in Cosmology

  • Do you know the distant galaxies are actually moving away from us, and the farthest galaxies are moving away faster than the closer ones? Logically, does this mean all the galaxies started from one tiny central point? (Edwin Hubble, 1929)
  • Why did the new radio telescope make constant annoying hits in the 1960s? Was this device hacked by the aliens?
  • Can we calculate the age of the universe via the 1% static on your untuned TV? If we put together what we know about how fast it expands today and how fast this stuff falls apart, what actually exists in the universe, and Einstein's Equations of General Relativity, can we rewind the history of our universe backwards?
  • If matter and energy only account for about 5% of the universe's total mass, what about the remaining 95%?
  • After 3 billion years, what will happen to the sun? (Guess where his coat goes… LMAO)
